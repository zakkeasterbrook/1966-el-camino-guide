Quick overview

  • Platform: Chevelle A-body (116 in wheelbase)
  • Production: ≈35,000 units across U.S. plants
  • Payload: 1,500 lb (when ordered with HD springs)
  • Design notes: Coke-bottle quarters, stacked headlights, SS396 styling cues

1966 brought the Chevelle SS look to the utility coupe: sculpted flanks, a wider grille, and the option to spec the brutal L78 375‑hp 396. Bucket seats with console, Strato benches, and color-keyed vinyl trimmed out the cab while the bed kept its full pickup practicality.

Powertrains & chassis specs

Inline Six

  • 194ci · 120 hp · 1-bbl · Base engine (RPO L22)
  • 230ci · 140 hp · 1-bbl · Smoother torque curve

Small-Block V8

  • 283ci · 195–220 hp · 2-bbl/4-bbl options
  • 327ci · 275 hp (L30) or 325 hp (L79 solid lifter)

Big-Block V8

  • 396ci L35 · 325 hp · hydraulic cam
  • 396ci L34 · 360 hp · large-valve heads
  • 396ci L78 · 375 hp · 11.0:1 compression · solid lifter

Transmissions

  • 3-speed column (standard)
  • M20/M21 4-speed with Hurst linkage
  • Powerglide 2-speed auto
  • Turbo Hydra-Matic 400 (behind 396)

Suspension: Coil springs all around; boxed rear arms on HD & SS packages.

Brakes: 9.5" drums standard, finned metallic shoes optional. Front discs arrive for 1967.

Rear axle: 10-bolt standard, 12-bolt on 396/higher GVW.

VIN & trim decoding

VIN format

Example: 136806Z123456

  • 1 = Chevrolet division
  • 36 = El Camino V8 (33 = six-cylinder)
  • 80 = El Camino body
  • 6 = 1966 model year
  • Z = Fremont, CA (B=Baltimore, K=Kansas City, L=Los Angeles, etc.)
  • Last six digits = sequence number

Cowl/trim tag callouts

  • ST 66-13480 = 1966 El Camino V8
  • TR 761 = black vinyl bench (check TR charts for color)
  • PAINT YY = Crocus Yellow (see paint codes below)
  • 2K = accessory group (e.g., A/C, seat belts, tinted glass)

Paint & option codes

Paint colors

  • A= Tuxedo Black · C= Ermine White · E= Marina Blue · F= Astro Blue
  • L= Artesian Turquoise · N= Madeira Maroon · R= Regal Red
  • V= Aztec Bronze · Y= Crocus/Cameo Beige · Z= Mist Blue

Hot RPO codes

  • Z25 — SS396 appearance package
  • L78 — 396/375 hp; solid lifter
  • M20/M21 — 4-speed transmissions
  • A51 — Strato bucket seats
  • N40 — Power steering
  • VO1 — Heavy-duty radiator (must-have for big-blocks)

Buying & restoration checklist

  1. Frame rails & rear body mounts: look for rust where the bed floor meets the kick-up.
  2. Bed floor & tailgate seams: factory seam sealer dries out—inspect underneath the stainless trim.
  3. Verify SS claims: Z25 is appearance only. For real 396 power, check engine suffix stamps and 12-bolt rear.
  4. Interior trim scarcity: Custom door panels/console are 1-year-only; repros exist but budget accordingly.
  5. Electrical upgrades: HEI and relayed headlights dramatically improve real-world drivability.
  6. Cooling: Use 4-core radiators + shroud for big-block/AC trucks—especially in modern traffic.
